NexCen QSR

NexCen POS provides a complete restaurant management solution designed for quick service restaurants like pizza-chains, ice-cream parlours, and caf√©s. Efficiently manages both billing and kitchen level inventory operations.

Features of NexCen QSR Software:

Billing and Invoicing
  • Touch based POS
  • Multiple Bill Holding
  • Remote KOT Printing / Kitchen Display System
  • Dynamic Pricing (Open Code/Items)
  • Dual Coding System
  • Multiple Mode of Settlement
  • User Definable Bill Format
  • Line Item Discount/Flat Discount
  • Day-wise / Date-wise Rates
  • Happy Hour/Promotional Schemes
  • Customised Tax Structure
  • Email Audit Trails (pdf/xls/doc formats)
  • Secure Crew Level Masters
  • Menu Item Modifiers
  • Dynamic Combos
  • Export of sales data to Head Office.
Delivery Management
  • Greet customers for birthday and anniversary
  • Complaint Managment System
  • Rider Management System
  • Recommendations based on HD History
Inventory and Food Costing
  • Dish Recipe
  • Item Wise Consumption
  • Transfer In/Out to another stores
  • Semi-finished Items
  • Variance Report
The following reports are generated from the system
  • Sales Summary, Sales Report
  • Sales Dish Wise, Cashier Scroll
  • Cashier Wise Sale, Total Sale
  • Daily Sales Summary (DSS)
  • Credit Card Report, KOT Reconciliation
  • Total Bill Printing, Audit Report
  • Expense Report, Hourly Sales Report
  • Complimentary Report
MIS Reports
  • Sales Tax Register, Credit Report,
  • ABC Report, Sales Analysis,
  • Cumulative Daily Sales Summary,
  • Time Wise Comparison, Home Delivery History
  • Graphics Report i.e. Pi Chart, Bar Chart etc.
All the reports can be generated for a Day / Week / Month or for any period. Reports can be on Screen, Printer or file. There is option to export the reports to Excel, Word and send through email.
