Spark Food-Court

Sparktech Systems Pvt Ltd (SSPL) provides the Food Court Billing Software with comprehensive features to cater to the food courts present in malls, educational institutes, corporate offices and entertainment complexes. A smart card is issued to the customer who can use it as plastic money to buy things from the multiple counters available in the food-court. Spark Food Court Software Solution easily manages cashier operations like issue of cards, recharge and refund of cards and the POS Operations for each counter. For each counter, Dish Masters are fed into the system and detailed reports are generated.

Features of Spark-Food Court Software:

  • Master
    • Counter Master
    • Card Master
  • Support single or multiple card counters for
    • Issue Cards
    • Recharge Cards
    • Refund Cards
  • Card Validity/Security can be configured
  • Centralized Cash Collection
  • Common Interface for Issue/Recharge and Refund
  • Management can track sales for all counters
  • Counter Billing/Adjustment
  • No scope of manipulation at counters
The following reports are generated from the system
    Cards Related Reports
  • Daily Collection/Refund
  • Card Utilization Report
  • Cards Issue Report
  • Cards Refund
    • Counter Sales Related Reports
  • Sales Report
  • Bill Reconciliation
  • Audit Report
  • Daily Sales Summary
  • Sales Tax/VAT, Service Tax Register
  • ABC Report
